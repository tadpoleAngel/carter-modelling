Carter Modeling

Carter Modeling provides custom 3D-printed scale models of residential homes, created from client-provided plans or reference materials.

Services

Each project includes digital 3D modeling and a physical printed model. Pricing varies by size and complexity; most models start around $100.

Delivery

Most orders are completed within 4–5 business days. Finished models are delivered locally or arranged for pickup.

Refunds

Because models are custom-made, refunds are generally not available once production has begun. Any issues with print quality will be addressed on a case-by-case basis.
